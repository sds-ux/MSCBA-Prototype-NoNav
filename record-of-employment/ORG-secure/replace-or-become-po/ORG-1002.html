Confirm your identity


Confirm your identity online through Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)

To confirm your identity online through the CRA, you must have a valid:

  • My Account for Individual with CRA; or
  • My Business Account with CRA.

By selecting “Confirm your identity with CRA” you will be redirected to the CRA website, where you will log into your CRA account and consent to share information with Service Canada to confirm your identity.

Information shared by the CRA

The information shared by the CRA:

  • will not be stored on your computer;
  • will not be altered in any way;
  • will not be retained if you close your browser;
  • will be retained by Service Canada and validated against your user profile.

Confirm your identity with CRA

Confirm your identity in person

To confirm your identity in person, you must visit a Service Canada Centre. You will need to bring 2 acceptable pieces of identification, 1 of which must have a photo. To facilitate the process please have your User Reference Number.
