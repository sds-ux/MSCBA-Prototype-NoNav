Add CRA Business

In order to add a Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) business, you must enter the 15 digit CRA Business Number. If you are a registered charity or a non-profit organization, please enter its registration number (e.g., 123456789 AB 1234). If a Payroll Program Account Business Number was provided with an RP extension, this should be used instead as it will speed up the confirmation process.

If the CRA business you are adding to your organization belongs to a different organization that is your client, please make sure the Records of Employment (ROE) Web Client Employer Consent Form has been completed and signed by both your organization and the client employer.

What is an Operating Name?

An Operating Name will appear as the name of your business on the Records of Employment (ROEs) that you will be issuing. If you do not provide an Operating Name, the CRA Legal Name will appear on your ROEs.


Overlapping ROEs

You may issue more than one ROE for an employee during the same period of employment if:

  • the employee has multiple positions under this CRA Business Number; and
  • it is recorded under different payroll reference numbers.
