Riley's Catering Inc. (ORG-185-584-321-165)

Record of Employment (ROE)

To issue an ROE, visit the ROE Web Main Page.

Register a New Organization

Register New Organization

Submit a request to register the organization you represent to the ROE web. Please ensure the organization is not already registered and that you are authorized to act as the account administrator (primary officer).

Replace the Primary Officer

Replace Primary Officer

Submit a new request to become the new account administrator (primary officer) for an already registered organization. Before submitting a request, ensure you are authorized to replace the current primary officer.

My Role and Administrative Rights

My Role

Use this option to view your role and user rights for this organization. You are not allowed to modify your own rights. If you wish to modify your rights you must contact an authorized representative or the primary officer for this organization.

Manage Representatives

Manage Representatives

Use this option to invite or terminate representatives' access from the organization's account or to modify other representatives' rights.

Manage Folders

Manage Folders

Use this option to create, modify or delete folders. Folders allow the organizing or restricting access to the documents and forms submitted through the system.
