Edit Organization

This option allows the representative to manage the organization profile, address book, contacts and to add or remove CRA businesses.

Manage Organization Representatives

This option allows the representative to invite or remove representatives from the organization and to modify their access rights. Representatives can only delegate or remove rights they already possess.

Manage Folders

This option allows the representative to create, modify or delete folders. Folders allow organizing or restricting access to documents and forms submitted online.
