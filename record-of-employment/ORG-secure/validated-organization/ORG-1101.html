Record of Employment on the Web (ROE Web) lets you issue Records of Employment (ROEs) for your employees and complete administrative tasks to maintain your account, such as:

  • replacing the Primary Officer
  • updating the business address
  • managing other representatives

ROEs 


View, issue and/or amend ROEs for this organization.

Primary Officer 


Submit a request to register the organization you represent to the ROE web. Please ensure the organization is not already registered and that you are authorized to act as the account administrator (primary officer).

Position Title and Administrative Rights 


View your role and user rights for this organization and/or change your position title.

Only an authorized representative or primary officer of this organization can modify administrative rights.

Organization Profile 


The Organization Profile allows you to edit this profile, which includes the main address of your organization, as well as the number of employees within the organization. Additional business locations can also be added to this profile if required.

Representatives 


A representative is a user who is invited to the organization profile and can perform tasks on behalf of the primary officer, such as submitting ROEs. Here you can:

  • invite a representative
  • edit existing representatives and their admin rights

Contacts 


A contact is a person Service Canada may contact if there are questions concerning the information on the ROE.

Folders 


Organize or limit access to ROEs submitted within your own organization by using folders.

User Access 


Manage User Access By CRA Business Number allows you to view a list of users within a specific business number and assign/remove access.
