Issuing a Record of Employment (ROE) 


View, issue and amend ROEs within this organization (depending on your level of access).

Register a New Organization 


Submit a request to register the organization you represent to the ROE web. Please ensure the organization is not already registered and that you are authorized to act as the account administrator (primary officer).

Replace the Primary Officer 


Submit a new request to become the new account administrator (primary officer) for an already registered organization. Before submitting a request, ensure you are authorized to replace the current primary officer.

View my Role and Administrative Rights 


Use this option to view your role and user rights for an organization. You can also change the title of the position you hold now.

Note that you are not allowed to modify your own rights. If you wish to modify your rights you must contact an authorized representative or the primary officer for this organization.

Organization Profile 


Within your Organization Profile, you can:

  • Edit Organization Profile, which allows you to:
    • update the Number of Employees within the Organization; and/or
    • update the main address for your organization.
  • Manage Address, which allows you to add or remove multiple addresses for your Organization.
    Why add additional addresses?

    Adding additional addresses is particularly helpful when creating ROEs for employees of an organization which has multiple locations. When completing an ROE you will be able to select from this list of addresses to specify the appropriate employer address on the ROE.

    Note that you must set 1 of the given addresses as the Main Address.

If your organization submits ROEs on behalf of other businesses, you can add their Business Numbers to your organization as needed by selecting Manage CRA Businesses.

Manage Representatives 


This option is only available if you are the Primary Officer or a representative authorized to manage other representatives. Representatives are people invited into the Organization to submit ROEs or to do other tasks.

Here you can:

  • view all of the representatives belonging to the organization;
  • invite new representatives;
  • edit user access rights of those representatives within the organization; and/or
  • remove representatives from the organization.

Organizing your ROEs with folders 


Folders allow you to organize, or restrict access to, ROEs submitted within your own organization. For example: your organization may have ROEs that it issues for regular staff and ROEs for executive level staff. By creating a folder for regular staff ROEs and a folder for executive staff ROEs your organization will be able to control which ROE Web representatives have access to either of these folders. This is especially useful if the earnings information on those ROEs is sensitive in nature.

Manage User Access 


This option is only available if your organization has many employees submitting ROEs and many business numbers to manage. Manage User Access By CRA Business Number lets you select a business number, view a list of users, and assign/remove access to that business number for each user on the list. Use this if your organization has many people submitting ROEs for many business numbers.
