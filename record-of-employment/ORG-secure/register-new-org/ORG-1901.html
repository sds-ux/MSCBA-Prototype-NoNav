Business owner

A Business Owner is an individual who is on file with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) and who has authority to provide consent on behalf of the organization for disclosure of their organization information from the CRA to Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC). Consent from a Business Owner is required to complete the registration process.

If you are not a Business Owner, the next screen will provide instructions on how to obtain the business owner’s consent.
