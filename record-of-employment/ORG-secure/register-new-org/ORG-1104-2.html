Register New Organization

Before you register

Before you register a new organization, you will need:

  1. your organization name and address
  2. the number of employees in your organization
  3. the 15-digit Business Number issued by CRA
  4. the organization's Legal Name as given to CRA
  5. the Postal/Zip Code associated with this organization at CRA
  6. if your organization submits Records of Employment on behalf of other organizations
  7. if your organization is a registered charity or a non-profit

Steps to Register

To register a new organization, you must:

  1. create the organization profile;
  2. confirm your organization;
  3. confirm your identity; and
    • Service Canada will send a letter to the CEO or owner of your organization. This will take 7 to 10 days.
  4. confirm your authority to act as Primary Officer on behalf of the organization

Once you create the organization profile and save, you will be able to access it at any time from the My Organizations page. The registration request will be complete only once all of the above steps have been completed and successfully processed. Service Canada may contact you at any time for clarification during the process.

For more information on how Service Canada protects your privacy, please refer to our Personal Information Collection Statement.
