Processus serveur : Envoie un couriel à l'utilizateur de MSCBA


Owner consent attempted

When the business owner access the owner consent process, notify the MSCBA user by email of the outcome. The MSCBA user views the email and can then login to MSCBA to continue the registration or to attempt to recover from an incomplete owner consent process.

Note: To simplify following the MSCBA user’s journey, we have added a link at the bottom of each of the emails. They are not part of the real emails but simply a convenience within this prototype. The links go directly to the appropriate MSCBA page without having to log in.

Owner consent given


When a confirmed owner chooses to end the registration or does not give consent, the system deletes the organization profile and the validation data. If the MSCBA user has confirmed their identity, the system retains that ID confirmation for further use. MSCBA does not stop users from attempting to registering organizations. To recover from this failure, the MSCBA user can attempt to register the same oranization if they think they can get an owner's consent.

Owner consent not given

Owner cancelled the registration


When we cannot confirm the owner, the system retains the all of the information collected for the registration for 2 months before deleting it. This time allows the owner to make sure CRA has their name as an owner and that the name attached to their CRA account matches the name they submit to MSCBA. The MSCBA user can also attempt to contact other owners (if applicable) and get them to access the owner consent process.

Owner identity not confirmed


Owner fails to act

When the business owner does not attempt to access the owner consent process, we expect the MSCBA user to follow up directly with the business owner. MSCBA does not send any email to the MSCBA user.

EWhen the business owner provides business number (BN) that does not match our records, MSCBA does not send any email to the MSCBA user.
