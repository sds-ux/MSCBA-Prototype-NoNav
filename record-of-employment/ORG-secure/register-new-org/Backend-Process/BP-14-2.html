Backend process: Process the owner's reasons


When owners decide to cancel the registration or choose to not consent, they must provide a reason for their decision. The system stores the reason in the BUS database and inserts it into an email to the MSCBA user that is registering the organization.

Because the owner does not want this registration to happen, the system removes all organization data from view (marked for deletion) for this registration, including:

  1. The organization profile
  2. The BN an other validation data

The only thing we retain is if the MSCBA user validated their identity. If users wants to recover, they must begin a new registration process.


The system closes the secure session and redirects the owner to the chooser page. You can follow the owner’s journey to the end by selecting continue.
