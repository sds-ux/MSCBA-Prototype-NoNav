Backend process: Send owner consent request email


When the MSCBA user is not the business owner, MSCBA sends an email requesting the owner’s consent to the MSCBA user, who then forwards the email to the business owner.

The rationale is to make the organization responsible for getting the owner to provide consent.

  1. If the owner does not take timely action, the MSCBA user or their manager can follow-up directly with the owner.
  2. If MSCBA were to send the email directly to the owner, then the MSCBA user would have to follow-up with the Employer Contact Centre (ECC). This becomes an expensive transaction for ESDC, and the ECC can do little to persuade the owner to take action.

Before leaving MSCBA, the user continues on to view a message that the consent status is now pending.
