Consent to share information with Service Canada

The Personal Statement of Identity facility is provided as a service to help you register with a government program that requires some personal information to authenticate your identity. This facility allows you to share some of the personal information you gave to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) with Service Canada (SC). Service Canada will use this information to authenticate your identity.

Please review the information listed in this statement, and if you agree with its accuracy, electronically sign this information and provide the CRA with your consent to direct this information to Service Canada. The information will be provided solely for the purposes of authenticating your identity and for no other purpose. The data may be stored by Service Canada for a period of time in accordance with all applicable laws and privacy policies.

Clicking on "I agree" below has the effect of electronically signing your information and providing your consent for the disclosure of that information to Service Canada, by the CRA, for the purpose of authenticating your identity. If you click on "I do not agree", you will be returned to the Service Canada website and the session will be terminated.

Additional security feature preference

If you select "Ask me a security question each time I login using this device," each time you login you will be asked to provide the answer to one of the questions that you chose.

If this is a trusted device and you choose "Do not ask me a security question each time I login using this device," a persistent cookie will be added to your browser. As a result, when you login you will not have to answer any of the questions that you chose.

Note

We recommend that you select "Do not ask me a security question each time I login using this device" only for your personal device and not one that is shared by others - for example, in Internet cafés or public libraries.
