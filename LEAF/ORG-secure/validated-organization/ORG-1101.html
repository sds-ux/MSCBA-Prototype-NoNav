Labour Electric Access Forms lets you issue Labour Electric Access Forms for your employees and complete administrative tasks to maintain your account, such as:

  • replacing the Primary Officer
  • updating the business address
  • managing other representatives

Labour Electric Access Forms 


View, issue and/or amend Labour Electric Access Forms for this organization.

Primary Officer 


Submit a request to replace the primary officer for an organization that is already registered. Before submitting a request, ensure you are authorized to replace the current primary officer.

Representatives 


A representative is a user who is invited to the organization profile and can perform tasks on behalf of the primary officer, such as submitting Labour Electric Access Forms. Here you can:

  • invite a representative
  • edit existing representatives and their admin rights

Position Title and Administrative Rights 


View your role and user rights for this organization and/or change your position title.

Only an authorized representative or primary officer of this organization can modify administrative rights.

Organization Profile 


View and/or edit your Organization Profile, as well as:

  • manage contacts
  • add, edit or delete addresses, including your main address
  • add other business locations
