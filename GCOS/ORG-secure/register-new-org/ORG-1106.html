Confirm your Organization


Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) issued a Business or Registration Number when you registered the Organization Legal Name. If CRA provided a Business Number with an RP extension, you should use that number instead as it will simplify the confirmation process. Service Canada may request supporting documentation to complete the validation process when you use a non-RP extension.

In order to confirm the existence of your organization, please enter the 15 digit CRA Business Number. If you are a registered charity or a non-profit organization, please enter its registration number (e.g., 123456789 AB 1234).

What is an Operating Name?

An Operating Name will appear as the name of your business on the Records of Employment (ROEs) that you will be issuing. If you do not provide an Operating Name, the CRA Legal Name will appear on your ROEs.
