Grants and Contributions Online Services (GCOS) allows you to apply for grants and contributions, and complete administrative tasks to maintain your account, such as:

  • replacing the Primary Officer
  • updating the business address
  • managing other representatives

Grants and Contributions Online Services 


View or apply for grants and contributions for this organization.

Primary Officer 


The Primary Officer is an individual responsible for the GCOS account on behalf of the Organization’s owner or Chief Executive Officer (CEO).

The Primary Officers responsibilities are to:

  • identify and authenticate other individual(s) as Representatives(s)
  • manage the Organization's GCOS account
  • accept the GCOS online agreement on behalf of the Business owner or CEO, and submit GCOS reports to the Government of Canada as required by law.

If you are authorized, you can submit a request to replace the primary officer for an organization that is already registered.

Representatives 


A representative is a user who is invited to the organization profile and can perform tasks on behalf of the primary officer, such as submitting grants and contributions. Here you can:

  • invite a representative
  • edit existing representatives and their admin rights

Position Title and Administrative Rights 


View your role and user rights for this organization and/or change your position title.

Only an authorized representative or primary officer of this organization can modify administrative rights.

Organization Profile 


The Organization Profile allows you to edit this profile, which includes the main address of your organization, as well as the number of employees within the organization. Additional business locations can also be added to this profile if required.

Contacts 


A contact is a person Service Canada may contact if there are questions concerning the information on the grants and contributions.
